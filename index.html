Methods

Costs

PV Cost = Σt=0..T-1 [(Fixed × multipliers) + (Per‑trainee × endorsed trainees) + (MoH slider cost)] /(1+r)t.

Benefits (₹)

Benefit = (Σ marginal WTP) × endorsement × stakeholders. (Treat as upfront; discount if modelling over time.)

QALYs

PV QALYs = Capacity × cohorts/year × years × endorsement × QALY/trainee, discounted. ICER = PV Cost / PV QALYs.

PSA

Draws from Normal(mean, sd=SE%×mean), truncated at >0. Recompute BCR, Net Benefit, ICER in each iteration; summary gives mean & 95% CI.
