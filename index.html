FETP India Decision Aid Tool

Configure Field Epidemiology Training Program (FETP) designs and instantly see stakeholder WTP, endorsement, realistic costs, and benefit–cost metrics (₹ & QALYs).

Why FETP? To meet India’s PM-ABHIM objectives, a skilled epidemiology workforce is crucial. This tool helps quantify value-for-money for scaling FETPs.

What you get:

  • Aggregate WTP (₹) from stakeholder preferences
  • Predicted endorsement probability
  • Dynamic annual programme cost & Benefit–Cost Ratio
  • Optional QALY-based cost-effectiveness outputs
