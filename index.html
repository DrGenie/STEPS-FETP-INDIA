FETP India Decision Aid Tool

Design and evaluate FETP configurations with stakeholder WTP, endorsement, costs, QALYs and PSA.

Purpose: Support decision‑makers to assess FETP expansion options using discrete‑choice evidence (WTP & endorsement), costs, and health‑economic outcomes.

  • Choose attribute levels (type, duration, focus, delivery, response time, capacity, cost).
  • Get marginal and total WTP; endorsement probability.
  • Compute present‑value costs, benefits (₹), BCR, and net benefit.
  • Estimate QALYs, ICER, compare to thresholds.
  • Run Monte Carlo PSA with uncertainty sliders.
  • Save scenarios and export to PDF.

Methods (Brief)

Costs

PV Cost = Σt[(Fixed × multipliers) + (per‑trainee costs × endorsed trainees) + (MoH training cost)]/(1+r)t.

Benefits (₹)

Total Benefit = (Σ marginal WTP) × endorsement rate × stakeholders (people whose preferences are represented).

QALYs

PV QALYs = Σt[capacity × cohorts/year × endorsement rate × QALY/trainee]/(1+r)t; ICER = PV Cost / PV QALYs.

PSA

Draws from Normal(mean, sd = SE% × mean), truncated to valid ranges (e.g., endorsement 0–1). Report mean and 95% CI for BCR & ICER.
