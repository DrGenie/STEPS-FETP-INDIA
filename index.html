FETP India Decision Aid Tool

Design and evaluate Field Epidemiology Training Programme (FETP) configurations using discrete‑choice evidence (WTP & endorsement), programme costs, QALYs and PSA.

What this tool does

Purpose: Support national / state decision‑makers to compare FETP expansion options on value (stakeholder WTP, endorsement), costs, health gains, and uncertainty.

  • Select attribute levels (type, duration, focus, delivery, response time, capacity, cost).
  • Obtain marginal & total WTP; predicted endorsement probability.
  • Compute present‑value costs, benefits (₹), benefit–cost ratio (BCR), and net benefit.
  • Estimate QALYs and ICER vs a threshold.
  • Run Monte Carlo probabilistic sensitivity analysis (PSA).
  • Save scenarios and export summaries to PDF.

Methods (Brief)

Costs

PV Cost = Σt[(Fixed × multipliers) + (per‑trainee costs × endorsed trainees) + (MoH training cost)]/(1+r)t.

Benefits (₹)

Total Benefit = (Σ marginal WTP) × endorsement rate × number of stakeholders represented.

QALYs

PV QALYs = Σt[capacity × cohorts/year × endorsement rate × QALY per trainee]/(1+r)t; ICER = PV Cost / PV QALYs.

PSA

Random draws from Normal(mean, sd = SE% × mean), truncated to acceptable ranges (e.g., endorsement 0–1). Report mean and 95% CI for BCR & ICER.
